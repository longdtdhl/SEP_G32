# Project Description

The Online Psychological Counseling Booking System (OPCBS) is a web-based platform designed to connect individuals seeking mental health support with certified professional psychologists.

The system serves as a centralized platform where users can discover psychologists, view professional profiles, schedule appointments, track consultation progress, access educational mental health content, and manage consultation packages.

For psychologists, the platform provides comprehensive tools to manage working schedules, appointment requests, patient records, consultation notes, blog content, and service packages.

For platform operators, the system supports doctor verification, content moderation, subscription management, analytics, and administrative operations.

The primary goal of OPCBS is to simplify the appointment booking process, improve accessibility to psychological counseling services, and provide a scalable platform for mental health professionals.

---

# Business Objectives

The objectives of the OPCBS project are:

* Provide an online platform for discovering certified psychologists.
* Enable guests and registered patients to book appointments conveniently.
* Support doctors in managing schedules and consultations efficiently.
* Facilitate secure patient record management.
* Provide educational mental health content through a blogging system.
* Support consultation package management and subscription services.
* Improve operational efficiency through automation and notification services.
* Deliver a secure, scalable, and maintainable software solution.

---

# Target Users

The system supports the following user groups:

## Guest

Unregistered users who can browse doctors, view blogs, create appointments, and track bookings using booking codes.

## Patient

Registered users who can manage profiles, book appointments, view appointment history, submit feedback, and purchase consultation packages.

## Doctor

Licensed psychologists who provide counseling services, manage schedules, review appointments, maintain patient records, create blog content, and manage consultation packages.

## Customer Support

Internal staff responsible for reviewing doctor applications and moderating blog content.

## Business Manager

Responsible for managing subscription packages, specializations, and business analytics.

## System Admin

Responsible for user management, role management, permission management, system settings, and platform governance.
