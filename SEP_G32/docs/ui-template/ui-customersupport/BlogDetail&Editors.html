In today's hyper-connected world, the constant stream of notifications and the pressure to be "always on" can significantly impact our mental well-being. Understanding how to navigate this landscape is crucial for maintaining internal peace.

The Impact of Screen Time

Studies have shown a direct correlation between excessive social media usage and heightened levels of anxiety. This is often driven by social comparison and the fear of missing out (FOMO).

  • Set specific "No Tech" zones in your home.
  • Practice mindful scrolling — ask why you are opening the app.
  • Use digital wellness tools to track and limit usage.

Finding a balance doesn't mean disconnecting entirely; it means choosing when and how you engage with the digital world on your own terms.
