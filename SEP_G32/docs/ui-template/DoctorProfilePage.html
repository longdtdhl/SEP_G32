Personal Bio

Hello, I'm Dr. Sarah Mitchell. My passion lies in helping individuals navigate the complexities of trauma and anxiety through evidence-based psychological practices. With over a decade of clinical experience, I believe in creating a sanctuary of healing where my clients feel heard, understood, and empowered to reclaim their mental well-being.

My approach is deeply collaborative and human-centered. I specialize in Cognitive Behavioral Therapy (CBT) and Eye Movement Desensitization and Reprocessing (EMDR), tailoring each session to the unique rhythm of your journey. Together, we will build a toolkit of resilience and find the path back to your most authentic self.
